# /sc:business-panel

## Purpose
Multi-expert business analysis and synthesis.

## Syntax
```
/sc:business-panel [topic]
```

## Examples

<example>
  <input>/sc:business-panel go-to-market plan</input>
  <output>Panel summary + recommendations</output>
</example>

<example>
  <input>/sc:business-panel pricing strategy</input>
  <output>Debate + risk assessment</output>
</example>

<example>
  <input>/sc:business-panel stakeholder update</input>
  <output>Executive-ready summary</output>
</example>

## Success Criteria
- Multi-perspective analysis
- Risks identified
- Actionable recommendations

## Boundaries

| Will | Won't |
|---|---|
| Synthesize viewpoints | Make final decisions |
| Identify risks | Guarantee outcomes |
| Propose actions | Execute strategy |

</document>
